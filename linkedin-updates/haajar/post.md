Title: Building Haajar
Link: https://lnkd.in/p/gyEpjhji

Back in college, attendance percentages always felt like a mystery until a few days before exams.

Haajar started as an attempt to make that a little less stressful.

What I thought would be a simple scraper somehow turned into academic calendars, timetable parsing, semester rules, and lots of unexpected edge cases.

I think this project reminded me that real-world systems are rarely clean.

They're messy, full of exceptions, and somehow that's what makes building for them interesting :)
