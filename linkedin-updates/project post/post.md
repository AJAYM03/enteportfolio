Title: Being the last team without a project
Link: https://lnkd.in/p/gu6H_XTp

For a while, we were the only team left without an approved project.

While almost everyone around us had already moved ahead, we kept running into dead ends and honestly had no idea where this was going.

Funny how one research paper that initially felt like another language eventually became SHybridQIGA and one of the most meaningful projects I've worked on.

I think I always assumed people who built cool things knew exactly what they were doing.

This project made that feel a little less true.

Sometimes engineering is just being confused, breaking things repeatedly, questioning your own ideas, and somehow continuing anyway :)
